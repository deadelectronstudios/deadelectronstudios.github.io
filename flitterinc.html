The year is 1982.

And you've just landed a job at Flitter — the hottest technology company on the planet.

As a Content Reviewer for Flitter, your job is to ensure that the micro-messaging platform remains friendly, respectful, and spam-free. But you're just a cog in the machine.
Primarly told through tweet-like micro-texts, Flitter Inc. is an experimental narrative game for Android that explores the ideas of social media, the future of humanity, and human agency.

As a Flitter Inc. employee, you'll have the chance to:
  • Explore a familiar yet strange world expressed through a thousand voices.
  • Experience gameplay that simulates the tedium of everyday office work.
  • Discover the truth behind the Flitter organization.
Developed by Dead Electron Studios
